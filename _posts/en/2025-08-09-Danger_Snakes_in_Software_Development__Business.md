# Danger: Snakes in Software Development & Business

> In extreme terms: there are **2 types of Snakes** that are extremely dangerous —
> for both Software Engineers and Businessmen.

## Types of Snakes

### 🐍 Highly Venomous Snake

_Highly Venomous Snake_ → kills quickly

> decisions that cause _immediate_ damage — fast and lethal.

examples:

- Business decisions without data validation
- Uncontrolled _scope creep_

### 🐍 Constricting Snake

_Constricting Snake_ → kills slowly

> problems that grow **slowly but surely** — by the time you realize it, it's already too late.

examples:

- Choosing the **wrong** _tech stack_ from the very beginning
- _Technical Debt_ that keeps piling up

## The Result in the end:

- `Wasted` _resources_: time, energy, mind, money
- A system that becomes increasingly harder to _maintain_

## Be Wary of Snakes

Software Engineers and Businessmen must be **extremely careful**:

> the type of Danger that kills immediately — Snake Venom
> the type of Danger that destroys slowly — the Constricting Snake.

Recognize the type of Snake early, before it's too late.
