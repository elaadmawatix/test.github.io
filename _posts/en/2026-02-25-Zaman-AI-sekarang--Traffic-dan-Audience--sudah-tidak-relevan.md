# In the Age of AI, Traffic and Audience are no longer relevant

I believe that in the age of AI, Traffic and Audience are `no longer relevant` when it comes to writing a blog.

## Why do I still write?

Everything is for 'Future of Me', as I wrote in the article [Writing for 'Future of Me'](/en/Writing-for-Future-of-Me/).<br/>
In that article, under the sections:

> <h2>Learned from the Internet, now sharing back</h2>
>
> I have learned a lot from the Internet {articles and videos created by many people}.<br/>
> I think it is time for me to start sharing my own writing publicly.
>
> <h2>Writing that is useful</h2>
>
> - useful for myself, in the "Future"<br/>
> - useful for others {hopefully}
