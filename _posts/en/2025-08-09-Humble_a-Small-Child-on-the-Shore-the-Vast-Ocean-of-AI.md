# Humble: a Small Child, on the Shore -- the Vast Ocean of AI

## Just a Small Child

> I am just a Small Child, standing on the shore<br/>
> Gazing at the Vast Ocean of AI that stretches before me

## Beautiful Pebbles

> Finding a few beautiful pebbles,<br/>
> slightly nicer than what my friends have found

## The Shoulders of Giants

> I am fortunate to see further into the Vast Ocean of AI<br/>
> because I am standing on the Shoulders of Giants
