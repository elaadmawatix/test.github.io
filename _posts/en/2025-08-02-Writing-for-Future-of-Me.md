# Writing for 'Future of Me'

I am a Programmer.<br/>
Age keeps adding up, year after year. 😁<br/>

**Problem:**

- too easy to `Forget`

The A Factor: Age / Time<br/>

😁😁😁<br/>
~~he he he...~~ ha ha ha...<br/>

{nuance — even laughter has its own cultural expression in writing! [English - Bahasa Indonesia]}

**Solution:**

- Write down everything that crosses my mind

**The idea:**

- for myself, in the "Future"
- later: just a quick glance at the documentation {I already wrote}
- and immediately, I understand the full context again

As a technical guide for writing, I created the article [How to write a .MD file](/en/How-to-write-a-MD-file/)

On the contrary — precisely because AI exists, I need to write even more.
